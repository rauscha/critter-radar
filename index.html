            Tap the circle to mark a critter fed today — it sinks to the “Fed today” pile and the tally (×N) climbs. It takes roughly 2–8 daily feedings to collect one as a companion (favourite food is faster), and the bar fills toward your goal. The fed check clears on its own at midnight, the same time the in-game feeding reward resets. Hit 🏆 once it’s a companion to file it away; restore it any time from “Collected.”

            Times are community-sourced (GameRant critter schedule, Dec 2025) and follow your device’s local clock. Two critters (Patterned Skunk, Blue Sweet Bee) also depend on weather, so they’re flagged 🌦. Your checks, tallies and collected list are saved on this device — use Export in Settings every so often to keep a backup.

            Fish are grouped by biome. The coloured dot tells you which ripple to cast at: white (common), blue (uncommon), gold (rare). Tap a fish for every spot it’s found. A few only bite at night or in the rain — those are flagged. Tap the circle once you’ve caught one to file it under “Caught.”

            Fishing spots are community-sourced (IGN, cross-checked against the wiki, 2026) and reflect your enabled biomes. Your caught list is saved on this device — use Export in Settings to keep a backup.
